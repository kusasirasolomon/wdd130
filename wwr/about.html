White Water Rafting Co.

At White Water Rafting Co., our mission is to deliver thrilling outdoor adventures and lasting memories for every client. Whether you're a first-time paddler or a seasoned explorer, we strive to make every moment safe, exciting, and unforgettable. Our guides are trained professionals who love what they do, and it shows in every smile, laugh, and high-five. Join us for the adventure of a lifetime — where the river meets fun and friendship flows freely!

History

Founded by river adventurers in 2005, our company has grown from one raft and a dream to a full team of thrill-seekers bringing joy to thousands every season.

Over the years, we've expanded our fleet, trained a world-class team, and built strong connections with nature lovers of all kinds. Our goal has always been the same — to provide safe, unforgettable, and joy-filled rafting experiences that bring people closer together and closer to the wild beauty of the river.
